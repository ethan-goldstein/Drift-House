Our Story

A seat is always waiting for you.

The Drift House was founded on one simple idea: bring elevated coastal dining to a place where everyone feels at home.

Inspired by the beaches of the Carolinas and the seafood shacks scattered along the Atlantic coast, we combine fresh ingredients, handcrafted cocktails, and warm hospitality to create memorable experiences — from casual lunches to celebrations that last all night.

Whether you're joining us for oysters at sunset, tacos after a beach day, or cocktails with friends, there's always a seat waiting for you.
